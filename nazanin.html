Venice Carnival

about festival

The Carnival of Venice is an annual festival held in Venice, Italy.
The carnival ends on Shrove Tuesday, which is the day before the start of Lent on Ash Wednesday.
The festival is world-famous for its elaborate costumes and masks.

more info location: Venice
Attendance: Up to 3 million
